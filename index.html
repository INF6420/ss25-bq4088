GRchive

Welcome to GRchive. This website is a virtual, digital museum of historical items from a personal, home-based archive of multiple collections maintained by the site's creator, who is both an enthusiast archivist and masters student in information science. The majority of the archival items represented here date from the 18th Century, with a concentration of items pertaining to the American Revolution. Item pages are continually being added, whether they represent new acquisitions to the collections or just new entries of preexisting collection items. As such, the historical scope of this archive as presented on this website is likely to expand although not lose its focus.

Items are organized by date and type and follow the Dublin Core categorization.

Enjoy your visit!
